# Frequently Asked Questions

## How often do you release episodes?

We release new episodes every Tuesday at 9 AM UTC. Episodes are typically 30-45 minutes long.

## Where can I listen?

You can listen on all major podcasting platforms including Apple Podcasts, Spotify, Google Podcasts, and Stitcher. You can also stream directly from our website.

## How can I get in touch?

You can reach us via email at contact@modernpodcast.example or follow us on social media. We read every message and appreciate your feedback!

## Can I sponsor the show?

Yes! We're always looking for sponsors that align with our audience's interests. Please email us at sponsorships@modernpodcast.example for partnership opportunities.

## Are there transcripts available?

Yes, full transcripts are available for all episodes. You can find them on the individual episode pages.

## Can I submit a topic or guest suggestion?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our listeners. Send your suggestions to suggestions@modernpodcast.example.

## Is the podcast available as video?

We primarily produce audio episodes, but we do share clips on our social media channels. Video versions may be available on our YouTube channel (coming soon).

## How long have you been podcasting?

The Modern Podcast launched in 2024 and has grown into a thriving community of listeners interested in technology and innovation.
