Design thinking isn't just for designers. In this episode, we discuss how product teams can adopt design thinking principles to create better products.

Key takeaways:
- Empathy and user research
- Prototyping and testing
- Cross-functional collaboration
- Failing fast and learning
- Scaling design thinking across organizations

Our expert guest will share real-world case studies from Fortune 500 companies.
